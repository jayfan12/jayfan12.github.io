原文
Note that in the statement of the general theorem on spreading speeds, it is often assumed that the initial data $u0(x) ≥ \sigma$ on a ball of radius $r\sigma$ . We prove that $r\sigma$ can be chosen to be independent of the positive real number $\sigma$ in the case where the monotone map $Q$ either is subhomogeneous or can be approximated from below by a sequence of linear operators.
